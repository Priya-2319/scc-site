Our Policies

Admission Policy

Admission is based on entrance tests conducted by the institute. Eligibility criteria vary by course. We maintain small batch sizes to ensure personalized attention.

All student data is kept strictly confidential. We never share information with third parties without consent. Our digital platforms use secure encryption.

Fees are non-refundable once paid. Installment plans available for selected courses. Scholarships offered to meritorious students.

Students must maintain discipline, respect faculty, and follow institute timings. Mobile phones prohibited in classrooms. Regular attendance mandatory.
